Day 1: Director's Pitch
Showcase your screenwriting skills by giving your own twist to well-known movies and TV shows and pitching them in front of an audience. You now have exclusive rights to have the ending that you always wanted!
Rules
  1. Every participant shall be allotted a movie or a TV show on random basis that cannot be changed or exchanged.
  2. The movie title shall be communicated to you via WhatsApp.
  3. Every participant must prepare a single slide ppt explaining the alternate plot, new characters, different theme of their assigned movie.
  4. Each participant shall be given 5 minutes to convince the audience that why they should watch that movie
  5. Judging shall be done on creativity, way of presentation and “Would you actually like to watch it?”
  6. The top participants shall then be qualified to participate and compete in Family Feud.
Day 2: Family feud
Inspired from the well known game show hosted by Steve Harvey in which five members of one family are pitted against five members of another family. Each team's goal is to guess the results of audience survey questions.
Rules
  1. The top performers from Director’s pitch shall be divided into teams.
  2. One member of each team faces the other in a face-off as the host reads the question off the game board.
  3. The team that buzzed in with the correct answer receives control of the board and has the option of playing or passing control to the other team.
  4. The team that has the control tries to reveal all of the correct answers to the question before receiving three strikes.
  5. If the team receives three strikes without clearing the board, control is passed to the other team.
  6. The team that now has the control is able to give one answer in the hopes that it is found on the board.
  7. If it is, points are added to the team's score.
  8. If not, the other team gets the points.
  9. Points are collected as each team finds its answers to the question on the board.
  10. Each team tries to collect the most points. The team with the most points wins the game!
